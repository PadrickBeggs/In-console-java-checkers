This is text based checkers, as most people can assume the best type of checkers.
This is how to control the checkers: 

Normal checkers are given 2 movement options: left (-1) and right (1)

Kinged checkers are given 4 movement options: left(-1) and down/up(-1 or 1) or right(1) and down/up(-1 or 1)

So when the game tells you it is your turn, it will take from the user a position on the board and how you would
like to move the piece. Given in this format. First, postion: x,y then, the displacement vector of the piece. 
for normal pieces: -1 or 1, for kings, its -1 or 1 then -1 or 1.
Player 1 is in control of red checkers while Player 2 is in control of black checkers. That is all so lets start!
